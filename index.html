Play Ground

Our school boasts one of the best playgrounds in the area. With a wide variety of equipment and activities, students of all ages and abilities can enjoy hours of fun and exercise.

Music and Dance

Our school is proud to offer one of the best Music and Dance classes in the area. Our highly qualified and passionate instructors provide students with a comprehensive education in the arts, covering everything from the basics of music theory to advanced performance techniques.

In our Arts and Crafts class, students have the opportunity to explore a wide range of mediums, including painting, drawing, sculpture, ceramics, and more. Our instructors provide guidance and instruction to help students create their own unique works of art.
